## Foundations of Web3

Before diving into coding, a solid grasp of Web3 fundamentals is critical:

- **Blockchain Basics:** Understand distributed ledgers, consensus mechanisms (Proof of Work, Proof of Stake), and trade-offs between scalability, security, and decentralization.
- **Cryptography:** Learn public-private key pairs, hashing, digital signatures, and zero-knowledge proofsâ€”core tools underpinning Web3 security and privacy.
- **Decentralization Concepts:** Comprehend decentralization beyond tech: governance, tokenomics, and community incentives.
- **Ethereum & Alternatives:** Ethereum remains dominant for smart contracts, but familiarity with Solana, Polkadot, and Layer 2 solutions is valuable.

Recommended Reading:

- [Mastering Bitcoin by Andreas M. Antonopoulos](https://github.com/bitcoinbook/bitcoinbook)
- [Ethereum Whitepaper](https://ethereum.org/whitepaper/)

## The Full Web3 Stack & Key Infrastructure Partners

Beyond smart contracts, mastering the full Web3 stack is essential:

- **Indexing & Querying:** [The Graph](https://thegraph.com/) powers decentralized data querying, enabling fast and reliable access to blockchain data. Monark is excited to potentially partner with The Graph to provide cutting-edge indexing solutions to our projects.
- **Infrastructure:** Reliable, scalable infrastructure underpins all Web3 applications. [Pinax](https://pinax.network/) offers robust, decentralized infrastructure services that help developers build resilient apps with ease.
- **Zero-Knowledge Technology:** Privacy and scalability often require advanced cryptography. [Midnight Network](https://midnightnetwork.io/) specializes in zero-knowledge proofs and privacy-preserving solutions, aligning closely with Monark's mission to advance secure, private Web3 systems.
- **Storage:** Decentralized storage protocols like [IPFS](https://docs.filecoin.io/basics/how-storage-works/filecoin-and-ipfs) and [Arweave](https://arweave.org/) for content persistence.
- **Wallet Integration:** [MetaMask](https://metamask.io/), [WalletConnect](https://walletconnect.network/) for user authentication and transaction signing.
- **Oracles:** [Chainlink](https://chain.link/), [Band Protocol](https://www.bandprotocol.com/) for connecting smart contracts with real-world data.
- **Scaling Solutions:** [Optimism](https://www.optimism.io/), [zkSync](https://www.zksync.io/), [Polygon](https://polygon.technology/) for Layer 2 performance improvements.
- **Frontend Libraries:** [Viem + Wagmi](https://viem.sh/), and tools like The Graph's subgraphs.
